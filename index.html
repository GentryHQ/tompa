Our Production Process

Discover the careful steps behind TOMPA's revolutionary production process

Washing and Cutting

This involves the careful process of Washing and Cutting. Our commitment to quality starts with the selection of fresh tomatoes sourced directly from the lush fields of Bomo community.

Dehydrating:

Once the tomatoes are washed and cut to perfection, dehydration takes center stage in our process, where specialized machines gently remove moisture. This step is critical in preserving the essential nutrients, taste, and vibrant color of the tomatoes.

Packaging:

The final step in our process is Packaging, where the transformed powdered tomatoes are carefully sealed in environmentally friendly, biodegradable bags. Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond the product itself.
